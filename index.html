Our Story

A Self-Financing Institution of Distinction

Established on January 3, 2010, our Mahavidyalaya stands within a sprawling 5-acre campus in Rampur Bawli Lalganj, Pratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh. Managed by a dedicated seven-member Management Committee.

  • Modern classrooms, fully equipped laboratories & workshops
  • State-of-the-art computer centre with high-speed internet
  • Well-stocked library with thousands of volumes & journals
  • Team of committed, experienced faculty members
  • Recognised under the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE)
  • Scholarship support via U.P. Scholarship & Fee Reimbursement portal

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To be a premier centre of learning and research in Uttar Pradesh, nurturing intellectually vibrant, morally grounded, and socially responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to a progressive India.

🎯 Our Mission

  • Provide quality, affordable higher education to all backgrounds
  • Develop critical thinking, creativity, and innovation
  • Promote research culture and evidence-based practice
  • Foster values of integrity, discipline, and social consciousness
  • Strengthen industry-academia collaboration for placement
