Our Story

An Autonomous Educational Institution

Established on 3rd January 2010, this college is situated on a vast 5-acre campus at Rampur Bawli Lalganj, Pratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh. It is managed by a seven-member Management Committee dedicated to quality higher education.

  • Modern classrooms, fully equipped laboratories and workshops
  • State-of-the-art computer centre with high-speed internet
  • A rich library stocked with thousands of books and journals
  • A dedicated and experienced faculty committed to student growth
  • Recognised by NCTE (National Council for Teacher Education)
  • Scholarship support available via U.P. Scholarship Portal

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To become a leading centre of education and research in Uttar Pradesh — building intelligent, ethical and socially responsible citizens who contribute to a progressive India.

🎯 Our Mission

  • Provide quality, affordable higher education to all sections of society
  • Develop critical thinking, creativity and innovation in students
  • Foster a culture of research and evidence-based practice
  • Instil values of integrity, discipline and social awareness
  • Strengthen industry-academia linkages for student employability
